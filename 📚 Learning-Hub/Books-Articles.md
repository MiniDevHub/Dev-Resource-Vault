## ğŸ’¡ MrDib's Reading Habits

### Effective Knowledge Absorption

- **Read consistently**: Even 15-30 minutes a day adds up.
- **Highlight & annotate**: Mark important sections, write notes.
- **Apply immediately**: Try out new concepts in code.
- **Discuss**: Talk about what you read with others.
- **Re-read classics**: Foundational books hold new wisdom each time.
- **Vary your sources**: Mix books, long-form articles, and quick reads.
- **Don't finish bad books**: If it's not engaging or useful, move on.
- **Summarize**: Try to explain the key takeaways in your own words.
