## ğŸ’¡ MrDib's YouTube Workflow

</div>

### Maximizing Your Learning

1. **Watch at 1.5x - 2x speed**: Most lectures are slow.
2. **Take notes**: Use Obsidian or similar for key takeaways.
3. **Code along**: Don't just watch, pause and type.
4. **Watch active coding channels**: Primeagen for raw coding.
5. **Use chapter markers**: Skip to relevant sections.
6. **Look at comments**: Often contain corrections or further insights.
7. **Subscribe to few, watch many**: Follow channels that resonate, but explore.
8. **Watch conference talks**: For deep dives and new trends.
