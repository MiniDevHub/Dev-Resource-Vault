### How to RTFM Effectively

1. **Start with the "Getting Started" guide**: Always the easiest entry point.
2. **Scan the "Concepts" section**: Understand the "why" before the "how".
3. **Use the "API Reference" for specifics**: When you know what you need.
4. **Look for "Examples"**: Often the fastest way to grasp usage.
5. **Check the "Troubleshooting" section**: Before hitting Stack Overflow.
6. **Contribute**: Found an error? Make a PR!

### Best Practices for Doc-Diving

- **Use browser search (Cmd/Ctrl+F)**: For keywords.
- **Bookmark important pages**: Keep your favorites organized.
- **Cross-reference**: Jump between different docs if needed.
- **Read release notes**: Stay updated with new features/breaking changes.
- **Playground/Sandbox**: Experiment with concepts directly from docs.
